Hamilton Center

Affectionately known as "Ham" by students, Hamilton Center hold's NCF's main dining cafeteria. It features an all-you-care-to-eat style meal with a range of options. Grab burger's, a salad,or one of many options today!

Boar's Head/C Store

Inside Hamilton Center, Metz offers a second location for students. Boar's Head aka the C-Store offers made-to- order subs, flatbreads, and smoothies. Here you can also find packaged snacks as well as convenience items.
